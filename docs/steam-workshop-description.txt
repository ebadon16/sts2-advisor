TITLE: Qu'est-ce Spire? — AI Card & Relic Advisor

SHORT DESCRIPTION: Real-time overlay that grades every card and relic, powered by community win-rate data. Like SpireLogs meets an in-game coach.

---

FULL DESCRIPTION:

[h1]What Is This?[/h1]
An overlay that appears on every card reward, relic selection, and shop screen — instantly showing tier grades (S/A/B/C/D/F), win-delta scores, and synergy analysis based on your current deck.

The advice gets smarter the more people use it. Every run uploads anonymized pick/skip data to a shared server, and the community-wide win rates feed back into everyone's recommendations.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] [b]Tier grades[/b] for all 400+ cards and 150+ relics across all 5 characters
[*] [b]Win-delta scoring[/b] — see how much picking a card improves your win chance
[*] [b]Deck-aware synergy analysis[/b] — grades shift based on your archetype (poison, strength, frost, etc.)
[*] [b]Community stats[/b] — aggregated pick rates and win rates from all players
[*] [b]Self-learning[/b] — your local play data blends with community data for personalized advice
[*] [b]Best pick recommendation[/b] with explanation
[*] [b]Deck breakdown[/b] — see your deck's archetype composition and balance
[*] [b]Draw probability[/b] — know your odds of seeing key cards
[/list]

[h1]Supported Characters[/h1]
[list]
[*] Ironclad (87 cards)
[*] Silent (88 cards)
[*] Defect (88 cards)
[*] Regent (88 cards)
[*] Necrobinder (88 cards)
[*] Colorless (64 cards)
[/list]

[h1]Controls[/h1]
[list]
[*] F7 — Toggle overlay visibility
[*] F8 — Toggle in-game grade badges
[*] F9 — Cycle panel position (left/right)
[*] F10 — Toggle deck breakdown
[*] F11 — Toggle draw probability
[/list]

[h1]Privacy[/h1]
Run data is uploaded anonymously (random player ID, no account info). You can opt out by setting CloudSyncEnabled to false in the settings file. The mod works fully offline — community stats just make it better.
